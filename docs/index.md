# Art Search API Documentation Test Site

## Introduction

Art Search lets you explore over a million artworks from museums around the world. You can browse paintings, sculptures, coins, furniture, textiles, and more — all in one place.  
The API makes it easy for developers to search and filter art by things like time period, material, artist, or style, so they can add this content into their own apps.  
Start exploring and bring centuries of art into your projects!

## Use cases

### 1. Educational & Academic Research  
**Dynamic Learning Tools:** Powering student apps that pull metadata (artist, period, medium) to illustrate history lessons.  
**Specialized Databases:** Allowing researchers to filter for hyper-specific criteria, such as "oil paintings from the 17th century made of ivory."  
**Art History Timelines:** Building visual maps that show the evolution of styles or a specific artist’s career over time.  
### 2. Creative & Lifestyle Apps  
**Inspiration Engines:** Creating "Art of the Day" widgets or mood-board generators that find art based on semantic concepts like "tranquility" or "chaos."  
**Interactive Quizzes:** Developing trivia games where users guess the movement, creator, or century using real museum-grade data.  
**Virtual Galleries:** Populating VR/AR spaces with high-quality images and accurate historical descriptions.
### 3. Museum & Archival Tech  
**Smart Museum Guides:** Enhancing visitor apps to display rich context when a user searches for or stands near a specific piece.  
**Catalog Enrichment:** Helping small galleries sync their digital archives with global metadata standards.
